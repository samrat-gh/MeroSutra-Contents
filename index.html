Getting Started with Rotational dynamics

Disclaimer: This blog strictly follows Grade 12 NEB syllabus (2078 / 2080).

Rotational dynamics is the study of motion of body in rotating motion. We have dealed with Linear/Translation motion in grade 11 which is the motion of a body in a straight line and now we study Angular motion which is the motion of body in a circular or spherical path.



The below table illustrates the linear motion's term into angular motion.
Example: linear displacement (s) in linear motion is called Angular displacement(θ) in Angular motion.

Linear Motion Angular Motion
Linear displacement(s) Angular displacement(θ)
Linear velocity(v) Angular velocity(θ)
Linear acceleration(a) Angular acceleration(θ)
Mass(m)[translatory inertia] Moment of Inertia(I)[rotational inertia]
Force(F)[translatory force] Torque(τ)[rotational force]
